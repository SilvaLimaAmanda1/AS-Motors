The Machines of AS Motor's.

ATZ-250: Gateway to the World of Supersport

The ATZ-250 is the perfect entry point into the world of sport bikes. Its 250cc single-cylinder engine is surprisingly agile and fuel-efficient, with a top speed of 170 km/h. The 12-liter tank makes it ideal for daily use, and its compact, aerodynamic design in vibrant lime green with black details ensures it stands out anywhere.

$14,900

GTX-500: Urban Agility and Sportiness

The GTX-500 is designed for riders who seek agility without sacrificing excitement. Its lightweight and responsive 500cc engine offers excellent cornering performance and a top speed of 190 km/h, perfect for city riding and winding roads. The 14-liter tank ensures solid autonomy, while its electric blue paint with white graphics reflects its energetic personality.

$15,000

VTX-R: Aggressive Lines with Refined Aesthetics

The VTX-R combines aggressive lines with a refined aesthetic. Equipped with an 850cc V-Twin engine, it delivers smooth yet powerful acceleration, easily reaching 240 km/h. Its 16-liter fuel tank allows for long journeys, making it ideal both on the track and the open road. The matte black finish with bronze accents highlights its sophisticated yet fast nature.

$25,500

AX-600: Elegance Forged in Speed

The Black Gold 600 fuses power and class into a single mechanical creature. Its 600cc engine delivers sharp bursts of acceleration and enough speed to take your breath away. The 16-liter tank ensures long rides without hesitation, while its deep black finish with golden accents shines like a jewel under the city lights. It doesn’t need to shout to be noticed — it commands respect with every curve.

$30,500

ASX-1000: Night Warrior in Two Colors

The Phantom Strike 1000 is the embodiment of modern power. With a 1000cc engine built to tear through the night, it breaks past the 300 km/h barrier as if it were just another step. Military green and deep purple give it a bold, almost untamable look. Its 20-liter tank fuels long, aggressive adventures. This is not just a motorcycle — it’s a presence. A swift shadow leaving trails of light wherever it goes.

$78,000

Speed 1400: Brutal Power and Cutting-Edge Technology

The Speed 1400 is the queen of speed. With a 1400cc inline-four engine, it delivers overwhelming performance, propelling the rider to speeds well beyond 300 km/h with ease. Its 18-liter tank is built to satisfy the thirst for adrenaline, while its dark metallic red body with silver accents leaves no doubt about its purpose: to dominate the track.

$90,900
